Our Mission

Bridging the Gap

We are building a world where excess food doesn't fill landfills, but fills stomachs.

"Hunger is not a scarcity issue, it's a logistics issue."

The Paradox of Plenty

It started with a simple observation in our local community: restaurants often prepare significantly more food than they sell. Due to unpredictable demand and stringent freshness standards, perfectly edible, high-quality meals end up in the trash at the end of every shift.

The Data

Research indicates that nearly 40% of food produced is wasted globally. In the restaurant industry specifically, surplus food accounts for a massive portion of this waste.

Our Solution

FoodBridge creates a digital highway between these two groups. We empower restaurants to turn potential waste into immediate aid, ensuring that surplus food reaches the plates of those who need it most, instantly and efficiently.
